T&M puts the risk on you. We take it instead.

Time and materials is the industry default not because it’s better for clients, but because it’s safer for vendors. When scope grows, hours increase, and the bill follows. Fixed price flips that dynamic — and it requires the vendor to know exactly what they’re doing.

We’ve done this before. Many times.

ZCLAP’s team has delivered Informatica MDM implementations across every major industry and project type. That depth of experience lets us price with confidence — we know what each element of a project costs because we’ve built it dozens of times. Our competitors estimate. We calculate.

Our methodology removes the variables.

Overruns happen when scope is undefined, risks are hidden, and assumptions go undocumented. ZCLAP’s implementation methodology is built to surface every complexity at the start — not midway through a build. Every entity, integration, and hierarchy is scoped precisely before a single line of configuration is written.

Fixed price creates the right incentives.

On a T&M contract, there is no financial incentive for your vendor to be efficient. On a fixed-price contract, there is. ZCLAP’s team is motivated to build right the first time, to make decisions decisively, and to deliver on time. Our business model only works if yours does.
